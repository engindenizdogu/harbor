# Joulin: Blockchain-based P2P Energy Trading Using Smart Contracts

IEEE Xplore | DOI: 10.1109/ISCC50000.2020.9219696
**Link to Article:** [ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9219696](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9219696)

**Key Features:**
- Engineered a blockchain-based peer-to-peer energy trading marketplace using Ethereum smart contracts, enabling trustless and efficient transactions with low costs
- Developed a proof-of-concept for the Joulin system, demonstrating its resilience, usability, and flexibility through quick response times and low transaction costs
